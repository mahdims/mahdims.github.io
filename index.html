Operations Research Scientist

AI & Optimization

I am an Operations Research scientist with a decade of experience in algorithm design (meta/heuristic, exact optimization) and machine learning methods to solve challenging business problems.

Currently, I am a Staff Scientist at the Huawei Vancouver Research Center and the AI Lead for the Huawei Optimization Solver (OptVers). I direct research efforts on AI integration with our solver to improve its performance and usability. You can see benchmarks for our solver's performance here.

Previously, I was a postdoctoral research fellow at the Department of Mathematics and Industrial Engineering of Polytechnique Montreal and CIRRELT under the supervision of Prof. Michel Gendreau and Prof. Teodor Crainic. I received my Ph.D. in Industrial Engineering and Operations Management from Koc University.

Research Interests

My current research interest falls into two broad topics:

  1. Using ML and optimization methods for practical applications, including last-mile and middle-mile logistics, production scheduling, and power distribution systems.
  2. Leveraging AI to enhance optimization methods, including using ML to improve heuristics and branch-and-bound, and applying LLMs for optimization modeling and interpretable OR.
